Dystopia Bakery Privacy Policy for Final Trial

At Dystopia Bakery, we respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting their personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game Final Trial.

Information We Collect

When you play Final Trial, we collect limited information from your device to ensure the smooth operation of the game. Specifically, we collect:

  • IP Address: When you start the game, your device sends a request to our server to check for game updates. As part of this process, our server logs your IP address. This information is used solely for the purpose of updating the game and is not used for any other purpose. All data is deleted after our server is restarted.
  • Game Version: We also collect the version number of the game installed on your device. This information is used to determine whether an update is available and to ensure that you have the latest version of the game.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the purpose of:

  • Game Updates: To check for and provide updates to the game, ensuring that you have the latest version and the best gaming experience.

Data Storage and Security

We store the collected information on our servers, which are protected by industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction of your data. However, please note that no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain the collected information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above. Specifically:

  • IP Address: We store IP addresses for a limited time, typically up to 30 days, for server maintenance and update purposes.
  • Game Version: We store game version numbers for as long as our server is up.

No Other Data Collection

We do not collect any other personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Device information (e.g., device ID, device type, operating system)
  • Location data
  • Personal identifiable information (e.g., name, email, phone number)
  • Behavioral data (e.g., gameplay habits, preferences)

Permissions

Our game Final Trial requires only the "Internet Access" permission to function properly. We do not request any other permissions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data collection and usage practices. We will post the updated policy on our website and Google Play Store page. Your continued use of the game after any changes to this policy will be deemed acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data collection practices, please contact us at dystopiabakery@gmail.com.

By playing Final Trial, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy.
